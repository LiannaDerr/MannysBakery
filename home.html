Welcome to Manny's Bakery, formerly known as Sunset Bakery!

Come down to Hope Street in Bristol, Rhode Island to try some of our famous Pasteis de Natas and original family recipes!

As a part of our commitment to sustainable development goals, we are fighting for zero hunger. Twice a week we take extra food, that would otherwise be wasted, and give to local homeless shelters and/or sell them to local grocery stores who then resell our bakery products for a low price.
