Our Policies

Financing: It can be difficult to properly manage cost allocation when it comes to perishable products and customer demand, but over the years and with almost a century-long experience within the family, we have made sure how to properly predict supply and demand and keep out business in good financial standing.

Delivery: We bake fresh everyday and make sure each batch is cooked to perfection. In addition to our stores, we also offer delivery services and catering!

Dissatisfaction: If there is ever a time a customer is dissatisfied with our food and our products or services don't meet expectations, we offer a full refund and extra pastries free of charge.
